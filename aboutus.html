About Us

The Abhaya Advocates was founded on August 21st, 2024, with a mission to empower women by providing seamless access to legal assistance, enabling them to protect their rights and seek justice. We believe in a world where every woman, regardless of her background, has the tools and support she needs to navigate legal challenges and advocate for her rights.

Mission

Our mission is to create a bridge between women and essential legal aid services, empowering them to take control of their legal battles. By offering easy access to legal resources, we strive to dismantle the barriers that often prevent women from seeking justice.

Vision

We envision a world where every woman feels safe, supported, and capable of navigating legal systems. Our ultimate goal is to ensure that no woman faces injustice alone, and that all women, regardless of their circumstances, have the knowledge, resources, and advocacy required to secure justice and protect their rights.

RightConnect Application

The centerpiece of our initiative is the RightConnect app, designed to provide women with legal consulting services remotely. This user-friendly platform offers personalized legal support through online appointments, 1-on-1 consultations, remote texting, video calls, and interactions in multiple languages, including through chatbot support. By making legal aid accessible and personalized, we are ensuring that every woman, no matter where she lives, can receive the legal assistance she deserves.

The Problem

Women across the world, particularly those from marginalized communities, face significant barriers in accessing legal aid. This includes limited awareness of their rights, a lack of affordable services, and concerns over confidentiality and safety. These challenges are even more acute for low-income women, women of color, and immigrant women, who often lack the resources and support networks necessary to seek help. As a result, many women are left vulnerable to exploitation, abuse, and violations of their rights, struggling to navigate complex legal systems alone.

The Opportunity

RightConnect was created to address these urgent needs. Our platform provides women with direct access to a network of trusted, vetted legal aid organizations and advocacy groups, helping them overcome the barriers to seeking justice. We offer a comprehensive, secure platform that prioritizes safety and confidentiality, empowering women to seek help with confidence. The app not only facilitates legal consultations but also connects women with the information and resources they need to advocate for their rights.
