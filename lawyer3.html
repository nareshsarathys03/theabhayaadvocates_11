Sneha Rao

Human Rights, Advocacy

Sneha Rao is an esteemed human rights lawyer with a deep passion for advocacy. She has spent over 12 years representing marginalized communities and advocating for their fundamental rights. Sneha has worked on high-profile human rights cases and has been a vocal advocate for equality and social justice.

Her practice focuses on ensuring that all individuals, regardless of their background, have access to legal protection and representation, particularly in cases involving civil rights violations and systemic inequality.
