Neha Patel

Employment Law, Workplace Discrimination

Neha Patel is an experienced employment lawyer specializing in workplace discrimination, harassment cases, and employee rights. With over 15 years of experience in the legal field, Neha has successfully represented numerous clients, ensuring their rights are protected and justice is served. Her dedication to advocating for equality in the workplace makes her a sought-after legal expert in employment law.

Neha is committed to providing personalized legal support to each of her clients, guiding them through complex employment issues such as unfair dismissal, gender discrimination, wage disputes, and more. She has a strong background in defending victims of discrimination and wrongful treatment in various industries, both in the corporate and public sectors.
